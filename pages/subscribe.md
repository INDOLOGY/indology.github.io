Subscribing to the Forum

If you wish to subscribe to the INDOLOGY forum, first

1. **Read the [Guidelines](https://indology.github.io/pages/guidelines.html)**.
   
   If you are eligible to join, according to the criteria described in the Guidelines,

3. **Send your short CV to the committee**

   The address is indology-owner@list.indology.info

   Without your CV, the committee will not process your application further. Your CV can be a single page,
   or a link to your university page if it shows your academic status and publications.

Thank you for sending your subscription request to the INDOLOGY committee.
